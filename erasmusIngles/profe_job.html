Job shadowing

  • 9 benefits of implementing Job Shadowing
    1. The worker recognizes whether he or she has the capacity to do the work
    2. Promote dialogue and encourage improvements
    3. Create interpersonal relationships
    4. Allows you to calculate the temporary cost of adaptation and learning
    5. Social skills are developed
    6. The new employee will practically know the position he or she is going to occupy.
    7. Integration of company values and philosophy
    8. Helps establish relationships
    9. Helps build networking
