Education system

Early childhood education center between 1 and 2 years old up to 6 years old. Primary education from 6-16 years Compulsory Education. Secondary education for ages 16-19 (and older). Adolescents between 16-18 years old have the right to receive secondary education.

The school year begins between August 23 and September 1 for both primary, secondary and high schools,

All education in Iceland is free. Several research centers and experimental institutions work in cooperation with the University.

Food

Icelandic hardfiskur, or dried cod, has been very popular among Icelanders over the centuries, as it is a healthy meal packed with protein and nutrients.

If you travel to Iceland in summer, you can see these puffins on the cliffs, as they are migratory birds that come to Iceland in the summer months.

Plokkfiskur is a fish stew and is usually on the menu of all restaurants. One of the main economic drivers of the country is fishing. Although a lot of fish is destined for export, the raw material stays in the country.
