Education system

The educational system of the Republic of Serbia is divided into Primary or Basic Education, which lasts eight years (from 7 years old in the first grade) and Secondary School, which lasts four years (from 15 to 19 years old).

The National Educational System is made up of the types: Basic, Higher Secondary and Higher, in school, non-school and mixed modalities.

Education begins in preschool or primary schools. Children enroll in primary schools at the age of seven. Compulsory education consists of eight primary grades.
