Education system

The Education System in the Nordic countries is very well-developed, which is why Denmark, Finland, Norway and Sweden have one of the highest literacy rates in the world. In the Nordic countries, secondary school (baccalaureate) lasts 9 years and is compulsory.

The Norwegian education system is predominantly public. Schools and Universities are run by public authorities, and education is free at all levels.

Food

Traditional recipes for typical Norwegian food are prepared with lamb, reindeer, fish, salmon, among others.

Whale meat is also eaten and dried salted cod is prepared accompanied by rice or vegetables.

The country's typical sweets are the Tilsorte Bondepiker, which is prepared in a transparent glass container with apples, whipped cream and breadcrumbs; plommegrateng, a fruit cake made with plums and a very light dough, which gives it a fluffy and airy texture when baked.
